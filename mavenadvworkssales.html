AdventureWorks Sales Analysis

This is the analysis of a extensive dataset of sales, customers and products provided during a MavenAnalytics course, constructed and designed in PowerBI Desktop.

Main objectives

This being the main project of the course, the idea was to utilize the vast majority of features in PowerBI Desktop to create the best dashboard possible, while learning most of the basic and intermediate features. This includes data sourcing, data modeling, data relashionsip management, dashboard design and usability, and optimization.

Executive Dashboard

The executive dashboard. Created to highlight high level information to allow better decision-making for company executives. The goal here is to highlight monthly KPIs, Top 10 products and product categories, trends and overall KPIs.

Product Detail Dashboard

This dashboard was designed to bring into light information aboutt a specific product. Monthly targets, trends and a insight summary for the selected product.

Customer Detail Dashboard

This dashboard presents the data about the customers of the company, bringing to light information such as top100 customers, orders by income level, orders by occupation, customer revenue trend and some insights about the top customer

Key Influencers

This is an extra dashboard where we can see what the data has to say about what influences the customer to be a homeowner.
